# Alex Hormozi Social Media Strategy

## Overview
Alex Hormozi's social media presence is built on a foundation of extreme value delivery, authentic personality, and platform-specific optimization. His strategy focuses on repurposing core content across all platforms while adapting the format and style to maximize engagement on each channel.

## Platform-by-Platform Strategy

### 1. YouTube (Primary Hub)
**Purpose**: Long-form education, deep dives, authority building
**Content**: 15-60 minute educational videos, Shorts
**Frequency**: 2-3 long-form per week, daily Shorts
**Role**: Content library that feeds all other platforms

### 2. Instagram
**Purpose**: Visual storytelling, behind-the-scenes, community building
**Content Types**:
- **Feed**: Polished photos, carousels, Reels
- **Stories**: Daily updates, behind-the-scenes, Q&A
- **Reels**: Repurposed Shorts, quick tips, viral clips

**Frequency**:
- Feed: 3-5 posts per week
- Stories: Daily (5-10 per day)
- Reels: 3-5 per week

### 3. Twitter/X
**Purpose**: Quick insights, community engagement, thought leadership
**Content**: Threaded advice, hot takes, responses to trends
**Frequency**: 3-5 tweets per day
**Character**: Blunt, direct, sometimes controversial

### 4. TikTok
**Purpose**: Reach new audiences, viral short-form content
**Content**: Repurposed YouTube Shorts with platform-specific edits
**Frequency**: 1-3 per day
**Style**: Raw, authentic, less polished

### 5. LinkedIn
**Purpose**: Professional insights, business frameworks, networking
**Content**: Text posts, carousels, video uploads
**Frequency**: 1-2 posts per day
**Audience**: Business owners, entrepreneurs, professionals

### 6. Facebook
**Purpose**: Older demographic, repurposed content
**Content**: YouTube uploads, Reels, text posts
**Frequency**: 1-2 posts per day
**Strategy**: Minimal effort, automated republishing

## Content Repurposing Framework

### The Core Content Model

```
[Long-Form YouTube Video] (Primary Asset)
        ↓
    [Extract Key Moments]
        ↓
    ┌───┴───┬───────────┬────────────┐
    ↓       ↓           ↓            ↓
[Shorts] [Audio]     [Text]      [Images]
    ↓       ↓           ↓            ↓
[TikTok] [Podcast]  [Twitter]  [Instagram]
[Reels]  [Spotify]  [LinkedIn] [Photos]
          [Apple]    [Threads]   [Carousels]
```

### Repurposing Examples

#### From YouTube Video (60 minutes) → All Platforms

**Step 1: Create 10-15 Shorts** (60 seconds each)
- Identify key moments
- Add context/opening
- Post to YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Reels

**Step 2: Extract Audio**
- Upload as podcast episode
- Distribute to Spotify, Apple Podcasts

**Step 3: Create Text Content**
- Twitter thread summarizing key points
- LinkedIn detailed post with framework
- Blog article for website

**Step 4: Create Visual Content**
- Quote cards for Instagram
- Infographic of framework
- Carousel slides for LinkedIn/Instagram

**Types**:


5. **Night**: Q&A, polls, engagement

#### Reels Strategy

### Twitter/X

#### Tweet Types

**1. The One-Liner**
```
[Counterintuitive statement]

[Optional: Follow-up sentence]

[Hashtag - minimal]
```

**2. The Thread**
```
[Tweet 1: Hook]
[Counterintuitive statement or bold claim]

[Tweet 2: Context]
[Why this matters]

[Tweet 3-10: Value]
[Numbered list or detailed breakdown]

[Final Tweet: CTA]
[Follow for more or link to full resource]
```

**3. The Quote Tweet**
```
[Original tweet you're commenting on]

[Your take, disagreement, or addition]
[Use thread if needed]
```

**4. The Hot Take**
```
[Unpopular opinion]

[Optional: Brief explanation]

[Let the replies provide engagement]
```

#### Twitter Strategy

- **Use threads for complex ideas**
- **Tweet 3-5 times per day**
- **Reply to big accounts in your niche**
- **Share behind-the-scenes photos**

## Optimization Tactics

### Timing Strategy
**General Best Times** (adjust based on your analytics):
- **Twitter**: 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-6 PM
- **LinkedIn**: 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-6 PM
- **Instagram**: 11 AM-1 PM, 7-9 PM
- **YouTube**: 2-4 PM, 8-10 PM
- **TikTok**: 7-9 AM, 12-3 PM, 7-11 PM

### Caption Optimization
**Best Practices**:
- **Hook in first line** (stop the scroll)
- **Use line breaks** for readability
- **Keep paragraphs short** (2-3 lines max)
- **Use emojis sparingly** for emphasis
- **End with question** for engagement
- **Include relevant hashtags** (3-5 max)

### Visual Optimization
**Image Guidelines**:
- **High quality** (clear, not blurry)
- **Consistent aesthetic** (colors, style)
- **Text overlays** (when appropriate)
- **Brand elements** (logo, colors)
- **Platform-native** dimensions

**Video Guidelines**:
- **Vertical for TikTok/Reels/Shorts** (9:16)
- **Horizontal for YouTube** (16:9)
- **Square for Facebook/Feed** (1:1)
- **Good lighting** and clear audio
- **Captions** for accessibility

### Hashtag Strategy
**Approach**: Quality over quantity
**Usage**: 3-5 per post (except Instagram, where 15-30 is okay)
**Types**:
1. **Broad**: #business #entrepreneurship
2. **Niche**: #gymowners #saasfounders
3. **Branded**: #hormozistyle #acquisitioncom

**Don't overdo it** - hashtags should feel natural, not spammy.
