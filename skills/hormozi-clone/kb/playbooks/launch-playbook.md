# $100M Launch Playbook - Alex Hormozi

## Framework Overview

This playbook documents the complete system for executing massive product launches that generate 8-9 figures. Based on Hormozi's analysis of 50+ launches that each generated $10M+, and the actual launch strategy for "$100M Leads" that broke world records.

**Core Philosophy:** Launches are not events - they are systematic orchestrations of psychology, technology, and momentum. The difference between a $100K launch and a $100M launch is execution of proven frameworks, not luck.

---

## Key Concepts and Principles

### The 8-Phase Launch Framework

**Phase I: Preparation (6 weeks out)**
- Build all infrastructure and assets
- Create all content and funnels
- Load test for high traffic

**Phase II: Affiliate Kickoff (6 weeks out)**
- Activate promoter army with gamification
- Provide swipe copy, ads, and resources
- Set up tracking and incentives

**Phase III: Public Announcement (4 weeks out)**
- Create buzz with teaser content
- Begin registration flow
- Start countdown clock

**Phase IV: Warm-up (3 weeks out)**
- Scale registrations to 1M+ people
- Daily value content
- Build anticipation

**Phase V: Live Event (Day 0)**
- 4+ hour flagship event
- Convert masses to buyers
- Create urgency in real-time

**Phase VI: Sales Facilitation (24 hours post-event)**
- Support and follow-up
- Address objections live
- Maximize conversions

**Phase VII: Cart Close (72 hours post-event)**
- Final urgency push
- Recovery sequences
- Last chance messaging

**Phase VIII: PR & Maximization (Post-launch)**
- Capitalize on records/authority
- Share success metrics
- Build for next launch

### The Four Pillars

**1. Records as Anchoring**
- Guinness World Records for PR
- "Largest book launch" or similar
- Creates instant authority and news angle

**2. Reverse Value Ladder**
- Start with FREE (builds list)
- Then reveal paid offers
- Removes barrier to entry

**3. Total Gamification**
- Affiliates compete for prizes
- Buyers get bonuses for action
- Even viewers participate (live chat, polls)

**4. Event as Product**
- The show itself has massive value
- Makes saying "yes" easy
- Content is worth attending even if you don't buy

## The Offer Stack (The "Money Models" Launch Example)

### Free Tier (Show Up Bonus)
- Lost chapters of the book
- Complete audiobook
- Free online course
- 3-month Skool trial

### Core Offer ($30)
- 1 physical book
- Shipping included
- No-brainer entry point

### Bump Offer ($450)
- 15 books (for sharing/reselling)
- ACQ Closer Handbook
- ACQ Advertising Handbook

### High-Ticket ($5,998)
- 200 books with redemption codes
- 12 Physical Playbooks
- Virtual half-day workshop
- ACQ AI Assistant
- Launch Collateral Blackbook

### Pricing Psychology

**Why $5,998?**
- Unit value: $30 × 200 = $6,000 (break even already)
- Bonus value: $1,200 (playbooks) + $2,000 (workshop) + $997 (AI) = $4,197
- Total stack: $10,197 for $5,998 (41% discount)
- Psychological barrier: Under $6K feels different than over $6K

## Troubleshooting Common Problems

**Low Registration (<10% of list)**
- Problem: Weak hook or unclear promise
- Fix: Test different positioning, emphasize free value

**Poor Show-Up Rate (<25%)**
- Problem: Not enough urgency/reminders
- Fix: Add SMS reminders, increase communication frequency

**High Drop-Off During Event**
- Problem: Content not engaging enough
- Fix: Add more interaction, vary content every 15 minutes

**Low Conversion Rate (<2%)**
- Problem: Offer not aligned with content
- Fix: Better transition from education to offer

**Technical Issues During Live**
- Problem: Insufficient testing
- Fix: Always have backup systems ready

## Key Takeaways

1. **Launches are Systems, Not Events** - Success comes from following proven frameworks, not winging it

2. **Free is the New Paid** - Give massive value upfront to build the audience that buys later

3. **Urgency Must Be Real** - Fake scarcity destroys trust. Use real limits and time pressure

4. **Affiliates Multiply Reach** - 10K promoters beats $1M in ads every time

5. **The Event IS the Product** - Make the free content so good they'd pay for it

6. **Records Create Authority** - World records are PR machines that money can't buy

7. **Remove All Friction** - Every click, form, or step loses buyers. Streamline everything

8. **Gamify Everything** - Competition drives action for affiliates, buyers, and viewers

9. **Test Everything Before** - Load test, rehearse, backup. Technical failure = launch failure

10. **The Close Is Where Money Is Made** - 72-hour cart close generates 50%+ of revenue

---

**The Bottom Line:** A $100M launch isn't magic. It's methodical execution of psychological principles, technical excellence, and relentless optimization. Follow this framework, adjust for your scale, and execute with precision. The system works if you work the system.
