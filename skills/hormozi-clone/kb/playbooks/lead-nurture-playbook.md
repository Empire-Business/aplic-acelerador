# $100M Lead Nurture Playbook - Alex Hormozi

## Framework Overview

**Lead nurturing is the difference between a 1% close rate and a 30% close rate.** Most businesses waste millions in ad spend by failing to properly nurture leads. This playbook, based on data from managing 4,000+ appointments per day through ALAN software, reveals the systematic approach to converting prospects into customers.

**Core Discovery:** The four pillars of lead nurturing are Availability, Speed, Personalization, and Volume. Most businesses get 1-2 right. The winners nail all four.

---

## Key Concepts and Principles

### The Four Pillars of Lead Nurturing

**1. Availability - More Time Slots = Higher Show Rate**

**The Data:**
- 10 time slots/day → 45% show rate
- 20 time slots/day → 60% show rate
- 30+ time slots/day → 75% show rate

**Why It Works:**
- Prospects book when convenient for THEM
- Reduces scheduling friction
- Captures momentum when interest is high
- More options = higher likelihood of booking

**Implementation:**
- Use automated scheduling (Calendly, Acuity, etc.)
- Offer 20-30+ time slots daily
- Include early morning, evening, weekend options
- Make it instant (no approval needed)

**2. Speed - Contact Within 5 Minutes or Lose**

**The Data:**
- Contact in 5 minutes → 80% conversion to appointment
- Contact in 30 minutes → 40% conversion
- Contact in 2 hours → 20% conversion
- Contact in 24 hours → 10% conversion

**Why It Works:**
- Interest decays exponentially with time
- Momentum carries through to action
- Being first matters (beat competitors)
- Professionalism signal

**Implementation:**
- Automated notifications for new leads
- Text + email immediately
- 24/7 response team (rotating)
- Template responses for speed
- "First responder wins" the lead

**3. Personalization - Generic Messages Die**

**The Data:**
- Generic template → 15% show rate
- Light personalization (name) → 35% show rate
- Full personalization → 55% show rate

**Why It Works:**
- Shows you care (not batch processing)
- Builds trust early
- Demonstrates attention to detail
- Creates relationship foundation

**Implementation:**
- Use their name (minimum requirement)
- Reference their specific situation
- Mention what they said/complained about
- Customize solution preview
- No "copy-paste detectable" messages

**4. Volume - More Touchpoints = Higher Conversion**

**The Data:**
- 1 touchpoint → 10% conversion
- 3 touchpoints → 20% conversion
- 5 touchpoints → 30% conversion
- 7+ touchpoints → 40% conversion

**Why It Works:**
- Most people don't see the first message
- Repetition builds familiarity and trust
- Different messages resonate at different times
- Life circumstances change (timing improves)

**Implementation:**
- Minimum 5 touchpoints over 7 days
- Mix channels (email, text, call, VM, social)
- Vary messages (not same thing repeated)
- Add value in each touchpoint
- Stop after 7-10 touches (no stalking)

### The BAMFAM Principle

**"Book A Meeting From A Meeting"**

Every customer interaction should end with booking the next interaction:

- Sales call ends → Next strategy call booked
- Strategy call ends → Onboarding call booked
- Onboarding ends → First check-in booked
- Check-in ends → Next review call booked

**Why Works:**
- Maintains momentum
- No "fall through the cracks"
- Shows you care (proactive)
- Advances the sale systematically
- Creates accountability

## Step-by-Step Nurturing System

### Phase 1: Immediate Response (0-5 minutes)

**Step 1: Automated Acknowledgment**
```
"Thanks for reaching out! I got your request and
I'm reviewing it now. I'll respond personally in 2 minutes."
```

**Step 2: First Personal Contact**
```
"Hey [Name], saw you requested [what they want].
I've helped [similar clients] with exactly this.

I have a few ideas for you. When's a good time
to chat? Here's my link: [scheduling link]

I have openings today at [3 specific times]."
```

**Key Elements:**
- Reference their specific request
- Show relevant experience
- Provide immediate scheduling options
- Make it personal, not automated

### Phase 2: The Follow-Up Sequence (Days 1-7)

**Day 1 (If no response):**
```
"Saw you haven't booked yet. Everything okay?

I know you're interested in [result]. I've helped
[multiple people] get exactly this.

Here are 3 times tomorrow that work for me:
[Time 1], [Time 2], [Time 3]

Which works for you? Or just pick a time here: [link]"
```

**Day 2:**
```
"Hey [Name], following up on my message yesterday.

I've been thinking about your situation with [problem].
I actually have a solution that's worked for [example].

Would you be opposed to a quick call? No pressure,
just sharing what's worked for others.

[Link to schedule]"
```

**Day 4:**
```
"[Name],

I know you're busy, but I also know this is important to you.

I helped [specific person] go from [before] to [after]
in just [timeframe].

I can probably help you too. Reply 'yes' if you want
me to share how, and I'll send my calendar link."
```

**Day 7:**
```
"Last message from me, [Name].

I genuinely think I can help you with [specific problem].

If you're still interested, my calendar is here: [link]
If not, no worries - I wish you the best in solving it."
```

### Phase 3: The Long-Term Nurture (Month 1-3)

**Weekly Value Emails:**

**Week 1: Educational Content**
```
Subject: 3 ways to solve [problem]

Hey [Name],

I know we haven't connected yet, but I wanted to share this.

Most people try to solve [problem] by [wrong approach 1],
[wrong approach 2], or [wrong approach 3].

But what actually works is [right approach].

I explain why in this short video: [link]

Hope it helps!

[Your signature]
```

**Week 2: Case Study**
```
Subject: How [person] solved [problem]

Hey [Name],

One of my clients, [Name], was struggling with [problem].

They tried [what they tried].

Then we implemented [solution].

Result: [specific outcome].

If you want similar results, let's chat: [link]

Best,
[Your name]
```

**Week 3: Soft Check-In**
```
Subject: Still interested in [result]?

Hey [Name],

It's been a few weeks since we first connected.

Just wanted to check: are you still looking to solve [problem]?

If so, I'd love to help. Here's my calendar: [link]

If not, no worries at all.

Best,
[Your name]
```

## The Appointment System

### Scheduling Best Practices

**1. Offer More Slots Than You Think You Need**
- Minimum 20 slots/day
- Spread across all waking hours
- Include weekends if possible
- Add buffer between calls

**2. Use Tiered Scheduling**
```
VIP Slots (limited):
- Best times (morning/early afternoon)
- Direct access to owner/expert
- Premium pricing tier

Standard Slots:
- Regular business hours
- Team member calls
- Standard pricing
```

**3. Instant Confirmation**
- No "pending approval" process
- Automated booking
- Immediate calendar invite
- Reminder system (24h, 1h before)

**4. Show Rate Boosters**
- Confirmation email immediately
- SMS reminder 24 hours before
- SMS reminder 1 hour before
- Personal video explaining value
- "Emergency contact" for rescheduling

### The No-Show Rescue

**If they miss the call:**
```
"Hey [Name], missed you today!

Everything okay? I know life happens.

I have these times available if you want to reschedule:
[Time 1], [Time 2], [Time 3]

Just let me know what works. I'll hold one for you."
```

**If no response to reschedule:**
- Wait 48 hours
- Send different approach
- Offer different format (email vs call)
- After 3 attempts, move to long-term nurture

## The Show Rate Optimization System

### Pre-Appointment Optimization

**1. Set Clear Expectations**
During booking:
```
"Great! Booked for [date/time].

Here's what we'll cover:
1. [Outcome 1]
2. [Outcome 2]
3. [Outcome 3]

Come prepared with [preparation needed].
I'll send a reminder 24 hours and 1 hour before.

```

**2. Send Value in Advance**
- 24 hours before: Educational content
- 1 hour before: Quick tip/value
- Makes appointment feel valuable
- Increases no-show penalty

**3. Use SMS Reminders**
```
"Hi [Name], looking forward to our call tomorrow at [time]!

Reply 'yes' to confirm you're still coming.
[Link to reschedule if needed]"
```

- Appointment booking rate

**Show Rate Metrics:**
- Overall show rate (target: 60%+)
- Show rate by lead source
- Show rate by time of day
- Show rate by day of week

**Conversion Metrics:**
- Appointment to sale conversion
- Time from lead to first contact
- Time from lead to appointment
- Touchpoints to conversion

**Pipeline Metrics:**
- Leads in each stage
- Average time in each stage
- Drop-off points
- Bottlenecks in process

**High-Ticket Services:**
- Show rate: 50-70%
- Conversion (appt → sale): 20-40%
- Response time: <5 minutes critical

**Professional Services:**
- Show rate: 60-80%
- Conversion (appt → sale): 30-50%
- Multiple touchpoints essential

**E-commerce:**
- Show rate: N/A (no appointments)

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Low Booking Rate (<20%)

**Possible Causes:**
- Not enough time slots available
- Scheduling process too complex
- Not personalizing outreach
- Competition beating you to response

**Solutions:**
- Double available time slots
- Simplify scheduling (one-click)
- Personalize every message
- Improve speed (automate)

### Problem: High No-Show Rate (>40%)

**Possible Causes:**
- Not sending reminders
- Not setting expectations
- Not enough urgency/value
- Too far in advance booked

**Solutions:**
- SMS reminders 24h and 1h before
- Send value in advance
- Book closer to call time
- Set clear agenda/expectations

### Problem: Low Conversion to Sale

**Possible Causes:**
- Wrong leads (not qualified)
- Not enough rapport/trust built
- Poor sales process
- Wrong offer or pricing

**Solutions:**
- Improve lead qualification
- More nurturing before ask
- Better sales training
- Review offer/price fit
