# $100M Goated Ads Playbook - Alex Hormozi

## Framework Overview

The Ads Playbook teaches a systematic approach to creating scalable advertising through **ad assembly** rather than creative "artistry." The core insight: most businesses fail at ads because they try to be creative rather than systematic. Hormozi's approach generated $100M+ through relentless testing and documentation of what works.

**Core Philosophy:** Ad creation is assembly - hooks, meat, CTA. Test 10x more than anyone else, document everything, and let data drive decisions.

---

## Key Concepts and Principles

### The Ad Assembly Formula

Every effective ad consists of three components:

1. **Hook (5 seconds)** - Stops the scroll
2. **Meat (15-60 seconds)** - Delivers value/persuasion
3. **CTA (5 seconds)** - Drives action

### The Five Ad Formats

**1. Demonstration Ads**
- Show the product/service working in real-time
- Example: "Watch me lose 5lbs in 7 days"
- Why works: Proof over promise

**2. Testimonial Ads**
- Customer results, not your claims
- Example: "How John made $50K in 30 days"
- Why works: Social proof builds trust

**3. Education Ads**
- Teach something valuable, then sell the solution
- Example: "3 reasons your ads aren't converting"
- Why works: Reciprocacy first, selling second

**4. Story Ads**
- Personal journey of transformation
- Example: "I was broke until I discovered..."
- Why works: Emotional connection

**5. Faceless/Pattern Interrupt Ads**
- Visual or auditory curiosity triggers
- Example: "You might be wondering why I'm holding a banana..."
- Why works: Brains can't ignore patterns

### The 70-20-10 Testing Framework

- **70% Core**: Proven winners you know work
- **20% Adjacent**: Concepts modeled from other niches
- **10% Moonshots**: Radical experiments

This mathematical approach (borrowed from Google's innovation strategy) ensures baseline performance while allowing for breakthrough discoveries.

## Step-by-Step Process

### Phase 1: Foundation (Week 1)

**Step 1: Build Your Hook Bank**
- Collect 50+ hooks from top performers in your niche
- Use tools like YouTube, TikTok, Instagram search
- Document in spreadsheet: Hook | Type | Views | CTR
- Categorize by type (Question, Statement, Story, etc.)

**Step 2: Document Everything**
Create tracking spreadsheet with columns:
- Hook/Ad Name
- Type (Demonstration/Testimonial/etc)
- Platform
- Views/Impressions
- CTR (Click-through rate)
- Conversions
- Cost per Lead
- Date

**Step 3: Test 10x More Than Competitors**
For each piece of content:
- Record 10 different hook variations
- Keep meat/CTA constant initially
- Test hooks against each other

### Phase 2: Implementation (Weeks 2-4)

**Daily Process:**
1. Morning: Review yesterday's top performers (10 min)
2. Create: 20+ new hook variations (30 min)
3. Test: Publish across platforms (20 min)
4. Evening: Document results (10 min)

**Weekly Process:**
1. Rank all hooks by performance
2. Identify top 10% and bottom 10%
3. Create 70% variations from winners
4. Add 20% from adjacent industries
5. Add 10% wild experiments

### Phase 3: Optimization (Month 2+)

**The "Let Data Teach" Protocol:**
- Never assume - always test
- Even bad content has a top 10% - find it
- Even good content has a bottom 10% - eliminate it
- Repeat forever

**Scale What Works:**
- Top hooks become permanent templates
- Build searchable database
- Create SOPs for team
- Adapt winning hooks across platforms

## Examples and Templates

### High-Performing Hook Templates

**For Cold Traffic:**
- "Real quick question... Can I have your email address?"
- "You might be wondering why [unexpected action]..."
- "Read this if you're tired of [pain point]"

**For Problem Awareness:**
- "If you're [situation], you're [doing it wrong]"
- "Business owners: Do you ever wonder if [doubt]?"
- "The smartest thing you can do today..."

**For Authority:**
- "I've been in business for [X] years..."
- "$[number] per month in [metric]..."
- "[Number]% of people never [result]. I've done it [X] times"

### Ad Script Structure

**Demonstration Ad (60 seconds):**
```
[0-5s] Hook: "Watch me get 50 leads in 1 hour"
[5-45s] Meat: Show exact process with timestamps
[45-55s] Proof: "Here are the results..."
[55-60s] CTA: "Click link below to get my system"
```

**Testimonial Ad (45 seconds):**
```
[0-5s] Hook: "How Sarah made $10K in her first month"
[5-35s] Meat: Sarah's story in her words
[35-40s] Proof: Screenshots of results
[40-45s] CTA: "Same system available here"
```

## Troubleshooting

**If CTR stays below 1% after 50 tests:**
- Your product/offer may be the problem, not hooks
- Survey audience about actual desires
- Test completely different hook categories

**If all hooks perform similarly:**
- You're not varying them enough
- Make differences more extreme
- Test completely different angles

**If performance is random:**
- Your tracking is broken
- Fix attribution before continuing
- Ensure statistical significance
