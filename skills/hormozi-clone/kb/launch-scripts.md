## Script Overview & Usage

### How These Scripts Work

**All Hormozi scripts follow a proven framework:**
1. **Pattern Interrupt** → Grab attention immediately
2. **Credibility & Authority** → Establish why listen
3. **Problem Identification** → What's the pain?
4. **Solution Reveal** → Here's the answer
5. **Proof & Results** → It actually works
6. **Objection Handling** → Address concerns
7. **Urgency & Scarcity** → Why act now
8. **Clear Call to Action** → What to do next

### Script Performance Notes

- **Conversational Tone**: Sounds like talking, not reading
- **Specific Numbers**: Uses real data, not vague claims
- **Story-Driven**: Uses examples and case studies
- **Objection-Aware**: Preempts concerns
- **Action-Oriented**: Every script has clear CTA

"On the thank you page, if you want to pre-order a copy of the book, I've got four other amazing bonuses for you there. You can upgrade to VIP and it literally is all free. If you just buy a book, it's gonna be awesome."

## Script Frameworks & Patterns

### The Hormozi Script Formula

**1. Pattern Interrupt Hook**
- Question format
- Unexpected statement
- Big number ($6M)
- Counterintuitive claim

**2. Rapid Credibility Building**
- Real businesses owned
- Actual results
- Specific numbers
- Portfolio companies
- Exit results

**3. Problem Identification**
- "Here's what I noticed..."
- "The thing that separates..."
- "What's interesting is..."
- "Here's why that matters..."

**4. Solution Introduction**
- "So I created..."
- "That's why I built..."
- "That's what this is about..."
- "Let me tell you about..."

**5. Value Stack**
- Physical items
- Digital access
- Community
- Coaching
- Intangibles

**6. Proof Elements**
- Case studies
- Testimonials
- Data points
- Before/after
- Specific results

**7. Objection Handling**
- Price
- Time
- Complexity
- Relevance
- Risk

**8. Scarcity & Urgency**
- Deadline (Monday)
- Limited quantity
- One-time offer
- Never again
- Disappears

**9. Risk Reversal**
- Tax write-off
- Resell value
- Guarantee
- Financing
- Payment plans

**10. Clear Call to Action**
- Hit the button
- Click below
- Do this now
- Simple steps
- What's next

### Psychological Triggers Used

**1. Curiosity**
- Secret project
- Mystery headliners
- Better than NFT
- Never before shared

**2. FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out)**
- 300,000 already registered
- 50/50 sellout risk
- Monday deadline
- Never doing again

**3. Social Proof**
- 500,000 projected
- 10 Disneylands
- One of biggest launches
- Video testimonials

**4. Authority**
- $250M portfolio
- Sold 3 companies
- 1,000+ consultations
- $31M in consulting

**5. Reciprocity**
- $6M invested
- Free bonuses
- 4 amazing gifts
- Better than promised

**6. Commitment**
- You're already registered
- You're gonna buy anyway
- You showed up
- You took action

**7. Scarcity**
- Limited seats
- Monday deadline
- One-time offer
- Never again

**8. Likability**
- Helping my warehouse guys
- Thank you from me
- I'm your biggest fan
- You're my people

**9. Consensus**
- Everyone's doing it
- 300,000 people
- Business owners like you
- People who move fast

**10. Loss Aversion**
- Worst case: tax write-off
- Keep the bonuses
- Resell the books
- Give them away

### Writing Style Characteristics

**1. Conversational**
- Sounds like speaking
- Run-on sentences
- Natural pauses
- "Like", "you know"
- filler words included

**2. Specific Numbers**
- $6,000,000
- 300,000
- 50/50 chance
- $31,000,000
- 226 businesses

**3. Story-Driven**
- "Here's why I did this..."
- "I'll tell you a story..."
- "Let me explain..."
- Case studies included

**4. Transparent**
- "Here's something I should confess..."
- "The real reason is..."
- "I'll be honest..."
- "Not gonna lie to you..."

**5. Self-Deprecating**
- "I know this sounds crazy..."
- "I probably shouldn't share this..."
- "This is probably insane..."
- Humor included

**6. Direct Address**
- "You're probably wondering..."
- "You might be thinking..."
- "If you're like me..."
- Second person throughout

**7. Educational**
- Teach the concept
- Explain the why
- Show examples
- Make it actionable

**8. Vulnerable**
- Share failures
- Admit mistakes
- Show learning curve
- Human element

- Keep the structure

- Track conversions
- Get feedback
- Optimize continuously

- Layer proof elements
- Stack bonuses strategically
- Create custom sequences

- Specific case studies
- Concrete examples

- Show the behind the scenes
- Admit what doesn't work
- Be human

- Real deadlines
- Actual scarcity
- Limited time offers
- One-time events

**4. Value Overwhelms**
- More than expected
- Stacked bonuses
- Risk reversal
- Multiple price points

**5. Community Amplifies**
- Affiliate incentives
- Social proof
- Network effects
- Shared mission
