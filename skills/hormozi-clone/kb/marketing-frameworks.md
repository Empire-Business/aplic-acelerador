## GOATED ADS FRAMEWORK

**What:** System for creating 150-750 winning ads per week

**Core Philosophy:**
"Most people test 1 ad. We test 150."

**The Process:**

**Step 1: Template Creation**
- Create ad templates (hooks, angles, formats)
- Systematize variation creation
- Build swipe files of winners

**Step 2: Mass Production**
- Batch create ad creative
- 150-750 ads per week
- Document every variation

**Step 3: Rapid Testing**
- Launch everything
- $50-100 per ad initially
- Let data decide

**Step 4: Scale Winners**
- Identify top performers
- Increase budget on winners
- Kill losers immediately

**Step 5: Iterate**
- Take winning hooks
- Create 10 more variations
- Test again

**Key Metrics:**
- Hook rate (first 3 seconds)
- Retention rate
- Click-through rate
- Conversion rate
- ROAS

**Target:**
- 36:1 ROI on lifetime ad spend

## HOOKS FRAMEWORK

**What:** System for creating hooks that stop the scroll

**The Formula:**
```
Call Out + Condition for Value = Hook
```

**Components:**

**1. Call Out (Identify the person)**
- "You're struggling to get clients"
- "If you're a business owner..."
- "Attention: [specific profession]"

**2. Condition for Value (What they get)**
- "...this will fix it"
- "...watch this video"
- "...here's the framework"

**Hook Archetypes:**

**Problem-Focused:**
- "Stop struggling with [problem]"
- "You're doing [thing] wrong"
- "The reason you're not [result]"

**Benefit-Focused:**
- "How to [result] in [timeframe]"
- "The framework that [result]"
- "Want [result]? Here's how"

**Story-Focused:**
- "I was [struggle]. Here's what I learned."
- "I lost [amount]. Here's how I came back."
- "They said I'd fail. They were wrong."

**Contrarian:**
- "Everything you know about [topic] is wrong"
- "Stop doing [common advice]"
- "The gurus are lying to you"

**Numbers-Based:**
- "7 lies about [topic]"
- "The 3-step system to [result]"
- "5 mistakes killing your [business/growth/etc]"

**The 80/20 Rule:**
"Spend 80% of your time on the hook (first 5 seconds).
20% on the rest."

**Why:**
If they don't stop, they can't buy.

## NURTURE SEQUENCE FRAMEWORK

**The 7-Day Email Sequence:**

**Day 1: Welcome + Quick Win**
Subject: "Welcome! Let's get you [quick win]"

- Deliver immediate value
- Give them a quick win
- Build trust

**Day 2: Story + Vulnerability**
Subject: "I was where you are..."

- Share your struggle
- Build emotional connection
- Show you understand

**Day 3: Framework + Value**
Subject: "The [framework name]"

- Teach something valuable
- Demonstrate expertise
- Make it actionable

**Day 4: Case Study + Proof**
Subject: "How [person] got [result]"

- Show someone else's results
- Build belief
- Provide social proof

**Day 5: Objection Handling**
Subject: "But what about [objection]?"

- Address common concerns
- Remove barriers
- Build confidence

**Day 6: Testimonial Stack**
Subject: "Real results from real people"

- Multiple success stories
- Overwhelming social proof
- FOMO creation

**Day 7: The Offer + Urgency**
Subject: "Last chance to [action]"

- Make the offer
- Add scarcity/urgency
- Clear call to action

**Key Principle:**
"The longer you delay the ask, the bigger the ask you can make"

## TRAFFIC HIERARCHY

**The Quality Pyramid:**

**Tier 1: Warm Referrals** (Best quality, lowest quantity)
- Conversion: 20-30%
- Source: Word of mouth
- Action: Maximize this first

**Tier 2: Warm Outreach**
- Conversion: 10-20%
- Source: DMs, network
- Action: Build relationships

**Tier 3: Cold Outreach**
- Conversion: 1-5%
- Source: Cold email, DMs
- Action: Systematize

**Tier 4: Content Marketing**
- Conversion: 1-3%
- Source: SEO, social, YouTube
- Action: Build long-term asset

**Tier 5: Paid Ads** (Variable quality, unlimited quantity)
- Conversion: 1-3% cold
- Source: Facebook, Google, etc.
- Action: Scale after validation

**The Golden Rule:**
"Start with Tier 1.

Work your way down.

Don't skip steps."

## A/B TESTING FRAMEWORK

**What to Test:**

**1. Hooks**
- Test 50+ variations
- Keep winners
- Kill losers

**2. Angles**
- Problem-focused
- Solution-focused
- Story-focused
- Result-focused

**3. Formats**
- Long-form vs short-form
- Video vs image
- Carousel vs single image

**4. Offers**
- Price points
- Bonuses
- Guarantees
- Scarcity elements

**5. Creative**
- Colors
- Images
- Copy length
- CTAs

**Testing Process:**

**Phase 1: Exploration**
- Test everything
- $50-100 per variation
- Let data guide

**Phase 2: Validation**
- Double down on winners
- Increase budget
- Test similar variations

**Phase 3: Scaling**
- All budget to winners
- Create similar ads
- Scale to limit

**The 70-20-10 Rule:**
- 70% budget to proven winners
- 20% to adjacent variations
- 10% to radical experiments

## REFERRAL FRAMEWORK

**Turning customers into salespeople**

**The System:**

**1. Deliver Exceptional Results**
- This is step 1
- Everything else depends on this

**2. Ask at the Right Time**
- Immediately after a win
- When they say "thank you"
- When results are achieved

**3. Make It Easy**
- Provide referral link
- Create email template
- Give them tools

**4. Incentivize Both Ways**
- Referrer gets reward
- Referee gets reward
- Win-win

**Example:**
"Refer a friend and you both get $100 off."

**5. Recognize & Appreciate**
- Public thank you
- Private gratitude
- Make them feel valued

**Target:**
- 30%+ of new customers from referrals
