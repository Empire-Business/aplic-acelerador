### 3. Upsell Offers
**File:** `02-upsell-offers.md`

**Core Upsell Types:**

#### A. The Classic Upsell
- **Concept:** "You can't have X without Y"
- **Example:** Rental car insurance, gas, late return
- **Strategy:** Solve the next problem immediately

#### B. Menu Upsells
- **Four Tactics:**
  1. Unselling (tell them what they don't need)
  2. Prescribing (tell them what they do need)
  3. AB offer (ask which they prefer)
  4. Card on file (make buying easy)

#### C. Anchor Upsells
- **Strategy:** Offer premium first (5-10x price), then main offer
- **Result:** More people buy main offer at higher price
- **Example:** $16,000 suit → $2,200 suit seems like a deal

#### D. Rollover Upsells
- **Concept:** Credit previous purchases toward next offer
- **Use Cases:**
  - Re-engage old customers
  - Rescue upset customers
  - Convert competitor's customers
  - Spread payments over time

**Key Metrics:**
- Upsells can increase profit 8-11x per customer
- Burger example: $0.25 → $3.00 profit through upsells

---

### 4. Downsell Offers
**File:** `03-downsell-offers.md`

**Core Downsell Types:**

#### A. Payment Plan Downsells
- **7-Step Process:**
  1. Reward prepayment (discount)
  2. Offer third-party financing
  3. Half now, half later
  4. Confirm interest (1-10 scale)
  5. Split into three payments
  6. Evenly spread payments
  7. Free trial with penalty

#### B. Trial with Penalty
- **Concept:** Free trial IF they meet your terms
- **Terms Examples:**
  - Attend all sessions
  - Complete homework
  - Attend check-ins (upsell opportunities)
- **Result:** Higher conversion than regular free trials

#### C. Feature Downsells
- **Strategy:** Change what they get, not just price
- **Options:**
  - Quantity: Less time, fewer items
  - Quality: Lower tier, slower response
  - Remove features: No guarantee, basic vs premium
  - Different format: DIY vs Done-for-you

**Key Insight:**
- Never offer same product for less (breaks trust)
- Always change something when changing price

### 5. Continuity Offers
**File:** `04-continuity-offers.md`

**Core Continuity Types:**

#### A. Continuity Bonus Offers
- **Strategy:** Give awesome thing when they join
- **Example:** Free $800 dog toys with $59/month food subscription
- **Pricing Rule:** Standalone should be 1.33-2.66x membership price
  - 1.33x = 50% choose continuity
  - 2x = 70% choose continuity
  - 2.66x = 90% choose continuity

#### B. Waved Fee Offers
- **Concept:** Month-to-month with fee OR waive fee for commitment
- **Example:** $5,000 setup fee OR free with 12-month commitment
- **Result:** People commit to avoid fees
- **Early Cancel:** Pay waived fee if they quit early

#### C. Continuity Discount Offers
- **Strategy:** Free time now or later for signing up
- **Combinations:**
  - Free trial → recurring
  - Prepay discounts (buy 5 months, get 1 free)
  - Lifetime discount for early commitment

**Key Benefits:**
- Sell once, get paid again and again
- More customers (lower barrier to entry)
- Long-term customer value

### 6. Make Your Money Model
**File:** `05-make-your-money-model.md`

**Three Stages of Money Models:**

#### Stage One: Get Cash (Attraction Offers)
- **Goal:** Turn strangers into customers
- **Metrics:** Cover acquisition costs
- **Examples:**
  - Win-your-money-back challenges
  - Free giveaways
  - Decoy offers (free vs premium)
  - Buy X, get Y free
  - Pay less now, pay more later

#### Stage Two: Get More Cash (Upsell/Downsell)
- **Goal:** Maximize 30-day profit
- **Strategy:** Solve problems revealed by first purchase
- **Tactics:**
  - Immediate upsells
  - Payment plan downsells
  - Feature adjustments
  - Menu options

#### Stage Three: Get Most Cash (Continuity)
- **Goal:** Recurring revenue + lifetime value
- **Timing:** Often after first 30 days
- **Methods:**
  - Membership bonuses
  - Prepaid discounts
  - Commitment incentives

## Real-World Examples

### Gym Launch Money Model
1. **Attraction:** Free books/courses → $16,000 licensing
2. **Upsell:** Gym Lords at $42,000/year (6k prepaid discount)
3. **Downsell:** $10k down + payments OR $800/week for 52 weeks
4. **Continuity:** Menu of services $50-800/week
**Result:** $4.4M/month in 20 months

### Myco-Gyms (Local Business)
1. **Attraction:** Win-money-back fitness challenge
2. **Upsell:** Supplement bundles personalized to goals
3. **Downsell:** Payment plans, smaller bundles
4. **Continuity:** $50/month discount with 12-month commitment
**Result:** Full gym + $20k/month recurring

### Newsletter (Digital Product)
1. **Attraction:** 30-day free trial
2. **Upsell/Continuity:** $297 now (lifetime rate) vs $399/month
**Result:** Combines 6 offer types in one

## Implementation Guide

### Step 1: Start with Attraction
- Pick ONE attraction offer
- Test until you get customers reliably
- Focus on covering acquisition costs

### Step 2: Add Upsells
- Identify problems your first offer creates
- Create solutions (more, better, new)
- Offer at moment of greatest need

### Step 3: Add Downsells
- Create payment plans
- Build trial options
- Develop feature tiers
- Goal: Turn "no" into "yes"

### Step 4: Add Continuity
- Membership bonuses
- Prepaid discounts
- Commitment incentives
- Goal: Recurring revenue

## Critical Principles

### Do's
✅ Perfect one offer at a time
✅ Measure in quarters, not weeks
✅ Raise prices gradually
✅ Use affiliate products to fill gaps
✅ Make continuity offers automatic renewal
✅ Combine offers creatively

### Don'ts
❌ Don't implement full model at once
❌ Don't offer same product for less (breaks trust)
❌ Don't create 100 products (create 100 offers for 1 product)
❌ Don't rush stages (build it right vs rebuild)

## Key Metrics

### Successful Money Model Indicators:
- **Minimum:** Make more from customer than acquisition cost in 30 days
- **Great:** Make enough from one customer to acquire 2+ customers in 30 days
- **$100M Model:** Make enough from one customer to acquire MANY customers in 30 days

### Pricing Guidelines:
- Upsell: 25-50% of customers should buy
- Downsell: Additional 25-50% conversion
- Continuity: 50-90% depending on price differential
- Payment plans: Should increase close rate without reducing paid-in-fulls

## Money Model Formulas

### Customer Value Equation:
```
Total Customer Value = Initial Sale + Upsells + Downsells + (Monthly Payments × Retention)
```

### 30-Day Profit Equation:
```
30-Day Profit = (Sale Price × Conversion Rate) + (Upsell × Upsell Rate) + (Downsell × Downsell Rate) - Acquisition Cost
```

### Scaling Equation:
```
Growth Multiplier = (Customer Value Increase) × (Acquisition Speed Increase)
```
- Double value + double speed = 4x growth
- Triple value + triple speed = 27x growth
