> "A money model is a sequence of offers designed to cover the cost of getting customers.
> So getting customers is no longer a constraint to your growth."

**1. Get people in the door (Acquisition)**

**3. Do it in less time (Speed)**

**4. Do it over and over (Retention/LTV)**
- Subscription models
