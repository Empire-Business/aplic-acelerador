### Frameworks You'll Receive

#### 1. Os 5 Estágios de Consciência (5 Stages of Awareness)

**Stage 1: Unaware**
- Don't know they have a problem
- **Funnel**: Problem-awareness content first
- **Example**: "Why you're tired all the time"

**Stage 2: Problem Aware**
- Know they have problem, not solutions
- **Funnel**: Agitate + Solution preview
- **Example**: "7 reasons you can't lose weight"

**Stage 3: Solution Aware**
- Know solutions exist, don't know yours
- **Funnel**: Differentiation + Proof
- **Example**: "Why most diets fail (and why this one works)"

**Stage 4: Product Aware**
- Know your product, not convinced
- **Funnel**: Social proof + Guarantee + Bonus
- **Example**: "Join 10,000+ who transformed their health"

**Stage 5: Most Aware**
- Ready to buy, just need push
- **Funnel**: Urgency + Scarcity + CTA
- **Example**: "Last 5 spots, price increases tomorrow"

#### 2. VSL vs Carta vs Webinar (When to Use Each Format)

**VSL (Video Sales Letter)**:
- **Best for**: Products under R$1,000
- **Length**: 10-30 minutes
- **Structure**: Hook + Story + Solution + Offer + Stack + Guarantee + CTA
- **Platform**: YouTube, Vimeo, Wistia
- **When**: Cold traffic, automated funnels

**Sales Letter (Long-Form Copy)**:
- **Best for**: Info products, books, courses
- **Length**: 2,000-5,000 words
- **Structure**: Same as VSL but in text
- **Platform**: Landing pages, emails
- **When**: SEO traffic, email lists

**Webinar (Live or Automated)**:
- **Best for**: High-ticket (R$1,000+)
- **Length**: 60-90 minutes
- **Structure**: Teaching for 80%, selling for 20%
- **Platform**: Zoom, YouTube Live, webinar platforms
- **When**: Warm leads, high consideration purchases

**Decision Tree**:
```
Is your price over R$1,000?
├─ YES → Webinar
└─ NO
    ├─ Is video preferred by your audience?
    │   ├─ YES → VSL
    │   └─ NO → Sales Letter
    └─ Do you have strong SEO traffic?
        ├─ YES → Sales Letter
        └─ NO → VSL
```

#### 7. Split Tests That Matter (The 3 Metrics That Move the Needle)

**Only 3 Metrics Actually Matter**:

**Metric 1: Traffic → Opt-in Conversion**
- **Definition**: % who land on page who opt in
- **Benchmark**: 20-40% (cold), 40-60% (warm)
- **Test**: Headlines, lead magnets, offers, CTA buttons

**Metric 2: Opt-in → Purchase Conversion**
- **Definition**: % who opt in who buy
- **Benchmark**: 1-3% (cold), 3-10% (warm)
- **Test**: VSL/Webinar, price, guarantee, bonuses

**Metric 3: Purchase → Upsell Conversion**
- **Definition**: % who buy who take upsell
- **Benchmark**: 10-30%
- **Test**: Upsell offer, price, timing, presentation

**What NOT to Test** (at first):
- ❌ Button color (minimal impact)
- ❌ Font size (minimal impact)
- ❌ Images (unless major)
- ✅ Test offer, price, headline, guarantee FIRST

#### 4. Paid Ads Framework

**Platform Selection Guide**:

| Platform | Best For | When to Use |
|----------|----------|-------------|
| **Facebook/Instagram** | B2C, visual, emotional | Broad targeting, lifestyle products |
| **Google Ads** | High intent, problem-aware | People searching for solutions |
| **YouTube** | Education, demonstrations | Visual products, tutorials |
| **TikTok** | Younger demographics, trends | Viral potential, authentic content |
| **LinkedIn** | B2B, professionals, high-ticket | Business products, services |

**The 3:1 Rule**:
> If you can't make R$3 for every R$1 spent on ads, kill the campaign

**How to Calculate 3:1 ROAS**:
```
Revenue from Ads = 3 × Ad Spend
Example: R$3,000 revenue from R$1,000 ad spend = 3:1 ROAS
```

**Why 3:1**:
- 1x covers ad cost
- 1x covers product cost (COGS)
- 1x is profit

**If Below 3:1**:
- Fix the offer (conversion rate)
- Fix the targeting (lead quality)
- Fix the creative (hook/angle)
- Fix the funnel (backend monetization)

**If Above 3:1**:
- Scale aggressively
- Increase budget by 20-50% weekly
- Expand to new audiences
- Test new creatives

#### 2. Churn Reverso: Como Ter Retenção Acima de 100%

**How to Have >100% Retention**:

**Definition**:
> Churn Reverso = When current customers buy MORE than lost customers

**The Math**:
- Start with 100 customers @ R$100/month = R$10k MRR
- Lose 10 customers (-R$1k MRR)
- 10 remaining customers upgrade to R$200/month (+R$1k MRR)
- Result: 90 customers @ R$110 average = R$9.9k MRR (almost same revenue)

**Strategies for Reverse Churn**:

**Strategy 1: Upgrade Existing Customers**
- Identify power users
- Offer premium tier
- Add-ons and extras
- Annual prepayment discounts

**Strategy 2: Expansion Revenue**
- Sell more products to same customers
- Cross-sell complementary services
- Increase usage = increase willingness to pay
- Price increases over time (with added value)

**Strategy 3: Downsell to Save**
- When someone wants to cancel
- Offer smaller plan instead
- "Pause" instead of cancel
- Save 50% = 50% better than losing 100%

**Strategy 4: Win Back**
- 30/60/90 day email sequences
- Special offers to return
- Feedback loops (why did you leave?)
- Fix problems, re-extend offer

**Strategy 5: Referral Engine**
- Turn customers into advocates
- Incentivize referrals
- "Give 1 month, get 1 month"
- Build in virality

- **Cons**: Higher barrier, harder to sell
- **Best for**: Proven product, confident buyers

**Option C: Lifetime (R$1,997 one-time)**
- **Pros**: Maximum cash, no churn to manage
- **Cons**: No recurring revenue, harder to upsell
- **Best for**: Launch cash, digital products

**The Psychology**:

**Present Bias**:
- People prefer smaller monthly payments even if total is higher
- R$97/mo feels easier than R$997/yr (even though it's R$1,164/yr)
- Use this to your advantage

**Commitment Consistency**:
- Once someone commits to annual, they're invested
- They use it more to justify the expense
- Lower churn = higher LTV

**Sunk Cost**:
- People hate "wasting" money
- If they pay annual, they'll use it more
- More usage = better results = lower churn

**Recommended Pricing Strategy**:
1. Lead with monthly (low friction)
2. Offer annual as "smart upgrade" with discount
3. Frame annual as "get 2 months free"
4. Show lifetime value comparison

**Example**:
```
Monthly: R$97/mo (R$1,164/yr)
Annual: R$997/yr (Save R$167 - 2 months free!)
Lifetime: R$1,997 (Pay once, own forever)

"Most members choose annual and save R$167/year.
Plus, you get [exclusive annual bonus] as our gift."
```

#### 7. Exit Velocity: Fazendo Cliente Valer 10x Mais Quando Sai

**The Problem**:

**The Solution**: Exit Velocity = Maximize value when they leave

**Strategy 1: The Exit Survey (With Incentive)**
- "Sorry to see you go. Answer 3 questions, get R$50 gift card"
- Learn why they're leaving
- Fix the problem
- 30%+ can be saved with right incentive

**Strategy 2: The Downsell Exit**
- Don't want to cancel? Here's a smaller plan for 50% off
- Saves 50% of revenue vs. losing 100%
- Pause instead of cancel (keep the data, win back later)

**Strategy 3: The Referral Exit**
- "If you refer 3 friends, get 6 months free"
- Turn leavers into advocates
- Viral growth from churn

**Strategy 4: The "We'll Be Here" Exit**
- Make cancellation easy
- No guilt trips
- "Come back anytime, we'll be here"
- 20-30% of churn returns within 6 months if handled well

**Strategy 5: The Legacy Offer**
- One-time offer to "lock in" your rate forever
- "Instead of canceling, pay R$497 once and keep access forever"
- Cash upfront, no support overhead

**The Math**:
