**Use this framework to structure your offer:**

```

M (Magnet): The [Unique Name] Method/System/Framework
A (Avatar): For [Target Audience]
G (Goal): Who Want [Specific Outcome]
I (Interval): In [Timeframe]
C (Container): [Program/System/Blueprint]

---

THE PROBLEM (Current Situation):
- [Problem 1 they face]
- [Problem 2 they face]
- [Problem 3 they face]
- [Problem 4 they face]
- [Problem 5 they face]

THE SOLUTION (What You Get):

Core Program: [Name] - $[Value] value
  - [Feature 1]
  - [Feature 2]
  - [Feature 3]

BONUS 1: [Name] - $[Value] value
  - Solves [specific problem]
  - Includes [specific deliverables]

BONUS 2: [Name] - $[Value] value
  - Solves [specific problem]
  - Includes [specific deliverables]
```

## SCRIPT TEMPLATES

### Sales Script Framework

```
**OPENING:**

"Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from [Company].

You requested information about [topic], correct?

[Wait for confirmation]

Great, I have about 5 minutes to share some details.
Does that work for you?

[Wait for confirmation]

Perfect."

---

**DISCOVERY:**

"Help me understand your situation better.

[Question 1 about current state]
[Question 2 about pain points]
[Question 3 about goals]
[Question 4 about timeline]
[Question 5 about budget - if appropriate]"

---

**PRESENTATION:**

"Based on what you shared, I can help.

Here's what we do:

[Explain solution - focus on outcomes]

Here's how it works:

[Step 1]
[Step 2]
[Step 3]

Here are results people like you get:

[Case study 1]
[Case study 2]"

---

**OBJECTIONS:**

[Address their specific objections - see Objection Handling Scripts]

---

**CLOSE:**

"Based on everything we discussed, does this make sense to move forward with?

[If yes]: Great, let's get you started...

[If no]: What concerns do you have?

[Address concerns]

Let's revisit the close - does this make sense now?"

---

**NEXT STEPS:**

"Here's what happens next:

[Step 1]
[Step 2]
[Step 3]

Any questions?"

---

**FOLLOW-UP:**

"I'll follow up with you on [day/time] to see how things are going.
Sound good?"
```

### Objection Handling Scripts

**"It's too expensive"**

```
"I understand budget is a consideration.

Let me ask you this:
What would it be worth to you to [solve problem/achieve result]?

Most of our clients find that [result is worth X times more than investment].

Plus, when you break it down:
[Price] ÷ [Months/Results] = $[X] per [unit]

And with our guarantee [restate guarantee],
there's no risk on your part.

Does that perspective help?"
```

**"I need to think about it"**

```
"Absolutely, this is an important decision.

Let me ask: What specific aspects do you need to think through?

[Address their specific concerns]

Is there anything else I can clarify that would help you decide?

If it makes sense to move forward, we can get started now.
If not, no pressure at all.

What feels right to you?"
```

**"I need to talk to my spouse/partner"**

```
"Of course, I think that's smart.

What information would be helpful for that conversation?

[Provide relevant details]

Here's what I recommend:
Share [specific result/case study] with them.

If they have questions, I'm happy to hop on a quick call with both of you.

When do you think you'll have that conversation?"
```

**"I can get this cheaper elsewhere"**

```

"You're right, there are cheaper options.

Here's why our clients choose us:

[Unique value proposition 1]
[Unique value proposition 2]
[Unique value proposition 3]

Most people who choose the cheaper option end up paying more in the long run
because [common problem with cheaper alternatives].

Our clients find that the value they receive far exceeds the investment.

Plus, with our guarantee [restate guarantee],
you're protected either way.

Does that make sense?"
```

**"I don't have the time right now"**

```
"I completely understand. You're busy.

Here's the thing: This program is designed to [save time/make time for you].

Our clients typically spend [X hours per week] and see [results in X time].

Plus, we have [time-saving feature/bonus].

Would it be worth [X hours] to get [result]?

If time is the issue, this is actually the solution, not another obligation.

What do you think?"
```

## CHECKLIST TEMPLATES

### Offer Creation Checklist

```
□ Define Dream Outcome (specific + timeframe)
□ List ALL current problems customers face
□ List ALL future problems your solution creates
□ Create solution for every problem
□ Name your unique mechanism (M-A-G-I-C)
□ Stack 5-10 bonuses (use Trim & Stack Matrix)
□ Calculate total value (10× price minimum)
□ Set premium price (2-3× higher than reasonable)
□ Create extreme guarantee
□ Add scarcity (real only)
□ Add urgency (real only)
□ Test offer with small group
□ Collect feedback
□ Iterate and improve
□ Launch to wider audience
```
