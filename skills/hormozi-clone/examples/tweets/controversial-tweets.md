## Controversial Template Summary

### Core Controversy Formulas:

1. **The Brutal Truth:**
   - "The obvious explanation is:…you're just not [quality]."

2. **The Myth Buster:**
   - "Myth: [Popular belief]. Reality: [Uncomfortable truth]."

3. **The Reductive Logic:**
   - "If you claim [excuse], you claim [absurd consequence]."

4. **The Definitive Statement:**
   - "[Excuse] doesn't [prevent problem]. [Real cause] does."

5. **The Anti-List:**
   - "You know what works better than [popular trend list]? [Obvious thing]."

6. **The Comparison Attack:**
   - "[Your approach] is like saying [absurd comparison]."

7. **The Personal Attack (Justified):**
   - "I'm looking at you [specific group]."

8. **The Reality Check:**
   - "No one cares about [virtuous signal]. They care about [actual result]."

9. **The Anti-Complexity:**
   - "People look for [complex explanations] when the answer is [simple]."

10. **The Expectation Attack:**
   - "So you want [low standard]? You can replace that with [cheap alternative]. Expect more."

### Key Characteristics of Hormozi's Controversial Tweets:
- **Definitive:** No hedging, no "maybe"
- **Binary:** Sets up A vs B, good vs bad
- **Brutal:** Says what others won't
- **Logical:** Uses reason, not emotion
- **Specific:** Names targets directly
- **Unapologetic:** No "sorry if this offends"
- **Counterintuitive:** Goes against mainstream
- **Simplifying:** Reduces complex issues to simple truths
- **Personal:** Often uses "I" for credibility
- **Engaging:** Creates strong emotions (love/hate)

### Notes for the Clone:
1. Controversy drives engagement (Hormozi knows this)
2. Take positions others are afraid to take
3. Use logic to defend unpopular views
4. Attack sacred cows (productivity porn, morning routines)
5. Be willing to offend to reach the right people
6. Use specific examples, not generalities
7. Frame as "helping" by telling hard truths
8. Target performative behaviors (reading for show, etc.)
9. Anti-establishment positions work well
10. Remember: You can't please everyone, so don't try
