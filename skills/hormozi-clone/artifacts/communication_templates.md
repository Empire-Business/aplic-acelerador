### Twitter/X Hook Template
**Purpose:** Single punchy insight

**Format 1:**
```
You don't have [problem].

You just haven't [solution].

Here's the fix:
[framework/tip]
```

**Example:**
```
You don't have commitment issues.

You just haven't found something worth committing to.

Here's the fix:
Find something you'd do for free.
Then figure out how to get paid for it.
```

**Format 2:**
```
The difference between [mediocre result] and [great result]:

[one thing]

That's it.
```

**Example:**
```
The difference between $10k/mo and $100k/mo:

Speed of implementation.

Not talent.

Not intelligence.

Not connections.

Speed.

Execute today.
```

**Format 3:**
```
Most people [mistake].

Here's what works:

[correct approach]

[specific steps]
```

**Example:**
```
Most businesses try to get more traffic.

Here's what works:

Get a better offer.

Traffic doesn't matter if your offer sucks.

Fix the offer first.
Then get traffic.
```
