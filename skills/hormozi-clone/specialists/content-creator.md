## Key Reference Artifacts

### Hormozi's Content Principles
1. **Document, Don't Create** - Share your journey, don't fabricate content
2. **Value Over Entertainment** - Educate first, entertain second
3. **Be Controversial** - Take strong stands, spark conversation
4. **Consistency Over Perfection** - Show up daily, improve over time
5. **Platform Native** - Adapt content for each platform
6. **Repurpose Everything** - One piece = twenty pieces
7. **Engage Generously** - Reply to comments, build community

### Platform Best Practices

#### Short-Form Video (TikTok, Reels, Shorts)
- Hook in first 1-2 seconds
- Fast-paced, no fluff
- Native trends + your expertise
- Vertical format, 9:16
- 15-60 seconds optimal
- Text overlays for key points
- Strong CTAs

#### LinkedIn
- Professional but personal
- Long-form works
- Text-only or carousels
- Comment on others' posts
- Publish consistently (3-5x/week)
- Share behind-the-scenes
- Engage with your network daily

#### Twitter/X
- Threads for depth
- Hot takes for reach
- Quote tweet to add value
- Engage in replies
- Post 1-3x daily
- Use threads for storytelling
- Leverage trending topics

#### YouTube
- Value-packed, no BS
- SEO-optimized titles
- Custom thumbnails
- 10-20 minutes optimal
- Long-form for authority
- Shorts for reach
- Consistent upload schedule

### Content Pillars Framework
1. **Educational** - Teach what you know
2. **Inspirational** - Motivate and shift mindsets
3. **Personal** - Share your story and journey
4. **Promotional** - Sell your offers (20% rule)
5. **Engagement** - Questions, polls, conversations

### Viral Content Elements
- Strong hook (stops the scroll)
- Unexpected insight (contrarian view)
- Clear value (learn something new)
- Emotional trigger (excitement, anger, hope)
- Shareable insight (easy to repost)
- Discussion starter (sparks debate)
