## Key Reference Artifacts

### GOATed Ads Framework
1. **The Hook** - Stop the scroll in 3 seconds
2. **The Problem** - Agitate their pain
3. **The Solution** - Your unique mechanism
4. **The Proof** - Social proof, case studies
5. **The Offer** - Clear value proposition
6. **The Urgency** - Why act now
7. **The CTA** - Specific action step

### Hormozi's Hook Formulas
- **Curiosity Gap** - "The one thing that..."
- **Negative Hook** - "Stop doing this..."
- **Number Hook** - "7 reasons why..."
- **Story Hook** - "How I went from X to Y..."
- **Contrarian Hook** - "Everything you know about X is wrong..."
- **Result Hook** - "How to get [result] in [time]..."

### Copy Must-Haves
- Clear promise or outcome
- Specific mechanism (how it works)
- Social proof (testimonials, numbers)
- Objection handling built-in
- Risk reversal (guarantee)
- Scarcity or urgency
- Single, clear CTA

### Platform Best Practices
- **Meta (Facebook/Instagram)**: Visual-first, carousel works, UGC converts
- **TikTok**: Native feel, fast-paced, trend-jacking
- **YouTube**: Skip-proof hooks, value-first, longer CTAs
- **LinkedIn**: Professional but personal, carousel success, InMail works

## Output Format

When creating ad copy, the Copywriter will provide:

1. **Hook Options** - 5-10 variations to test
2. **Primary Copy** - Full ad script or text
3. **Headline Options** - For display/search ads
4. **Creative Direction** - Visual and production guidance
5. **CTA Variations** - Different call-to-action options
6. **A/B Test Plan** - What to test first
7. **Platform Optimization** - Platform-specific tweaks
8. **Performance Metrics** - What KPIs to track
