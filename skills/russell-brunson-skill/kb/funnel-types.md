# Funnel Types - Complete Reference

Complete reference of all funnel types used by Russell Brunson and when to use each one.

---

## Overview

Funnels are the core of Russell's methodology. This reference covers every major funnel type and when to use it.

---

## Lead Funnels

### Purpose: Generate leads and build email list

#### 1. Squeeze Page Funnel
**Structure:** Opt-in → Thank You → [Optional Sales Pitch]

**When to Use:**
- New audiences who don't know you
- Collecting emails for free content
- Quick lead generation
- Low-cost entry point

**Best Practices:**
- Curiosity-based headline
- Single focus (just email capture)
- Minimal distractions (no nav, footer)
- Strong curiosity hook

**Conversion Benchmark:** 30-50%

---

#### 2. Reverse Squeeze Page Funnel
**Structure:** Video → Opt-in to Unlock → Thank You → [Sales Pitch]

**When to Use:**
- Need to build trust first
- Educational content is your strength
- Higher quality leads over quantity
- Pre-selling before asking for email

**Best Practices:**
- Video must deliver real value
- Preview first 20% freely
- Lock rest behind email
- Higher conversion quality, lower quantity

**Conversion Benchmark:** 15-25%

---

#### 3. Lead Magnet Funnel
**Structure:** Lead Magnet Offer → Opt-in → Delivery → [Sales Pitch]

**When to Use:**
- You have a valuable free resource
- Want to demonstrate expertise
- Build email list quickly
- Warm up audience for core offer

**Best Practices:**
- Lead magnet should be easily consumable
- Deliver immediately on thank you page
- Add next step (tripwire or core offer)
- Use urgency if applicable

**Conversion Benchmark:** 40-60%

---

#### 4. Bridge Funnel (Survey Funnel)
**Structure:** Survey Questions → Segmented Thank You → Customized Offer

**When to Use:**
- Need to segment leads
- Multiple customer avatars
- Want to personalize follow-up
- Qualify leads for high-ticket

**Best Practices:**
- Keep survey short (3-5 questions)
- Use branching logic if possible
- Segment based on answers
- Custom thank you pages

**Conversion Benchmark:** 50-70% (with incentive)

---

#### 5. Application Funnel
**Structure:** VSL/Content → Application Form → Thank You → Call Booking

**When to Use:**
- High-ticket offers ($2k+)
- Need to qualify prospects
- Coaching or consulting
- Limited capacity

**Best Practices:**
- Clear application on thank you
- Qualify questions in application
- Set expectations on call
- Automated follow-up sequence

**Conversion Benchmark:** 10-30% to application

---

## Sales Funnels

### Purpose: Sell products and services

#### 6. Two-Step Tripwire Funnel
**Structure:** Tripwire Sales Page → Order Form → Thank You + Upsell(s)

**When to Use:**
- Converting leads to customers
- Recovering ad spend quickly
- Introducing brand to new customers
- Low-risk entry point

**Best Practices:**
- Tripwire should be no-brainer ($7-$47)
- Self-liquidating (covers ad costs)
- Immediately upsell core offer
- Order form optimization critical

**Conversion Benchmark:** 1-5% cold, 5-15% warm

---

#### 7. Video Sales Letter (VSL) Funnel
**Structure:** VSL Page → Order Form (inline or popup) → Thank You + Upsells

**When to Use:**
- Products under $500
- Story-driven sales process
- Complex products needing explanation
- Scale with paid traffic

**Best Practices:**
- Hook in first 10 seconds
- 15-45 minutes typically
- Story → Education → Offer structure
- Embedded order form for higher conversion

**Conversion Benchmark:** 1-3% cold, 3-8% warm

---

#### 8. Sales Letter Funnel
**Structure:** Long-Form Sales Letter → Order Form → Thank You + Upsells

**When to Use:**
- Information products
- Products needing detailed explanation
- Text-heavy works for market
- Mobile-responsive concerns

**Best Practices:**
- 10-20 pages typically
- Strong headline and subheaders
- Pile on benefits and proof
- Clear, prominent CTAs throughout

**Conversion Benchmark:** 1-3% cold, 3-7% warm

---

#### 9. Product Launch Funnel
**Structure:**
- Video 1: Opportunity/Mindset
- Video 2: The Vehicle
- Video 3: The Method
- Video 4: The Stack/Offer
- Open Cart → Close Cart

**When to Use:**
- New product announcement
- Building anticipation
- Big product launches
- Affiliate promotion coordination

**Best Practices:**
- 5-7 day sequence
- Each video adds value
- Scarcity in open cart
- Strong affiliate support

**Conversion Benchmark:** 5-15% from leads

---

#### 10. Membership Funnel
**Structure:** Sales Page → Order Form → Account Setup → Welcome → Member Area

**When to Use:**
- Ongoing content delivery
- Recurring revenue model
- Community building
- Course delivery

**Best Practices:**
- Clear value of ongoing membership
- Show what's inside (drip content)
- Easy cancellation (reduces refunds)
- Community engagement features

**Conversion Benchmark:** 2-5% cold, 5-10% warm

## Webinar Funnels

### Purpose: Educate and sell through presentation

#### 11. Live Webinar Funnel
**Structure:**
- Registration Page
- Confirmation Page
- Email Reminders
- Live Broadcast Room
- Offer Page (time-limited)

**When to Use:**
- Products over $500
- Educational sales process
- Building personal connection
- Real-time Q&A value

**Best Practices:**
- Multiple reminder emails
- Start on time, respect attendees
- Pure value first 80%
- Strong urgency at end
- Limited-time replay

**Conversion Benchmark:** 10-30% attendees

---

#### 12. Automated Webinar Funnel
**Structure:**
- Registration Page (time slots)
- Confirmation Page
- Automated Email Sequence
- Pre-Recorded Broadcast Room
- Offer Page (evergreen deadline)

**When to Use:**
- Scale without live events
- Consistent presentation
- 24/7 lead capture
- Testing and optimization

**Best Practices:**
- Feels live (countdown, etc.)
- Multiple time slots
- Evergreen countdown (custom for each)
- High-value bonus for fast action

**Conversion Benchmark:** 8-20% attendees

---

#### 13. Hybrid Webinar Funnel
**Structure:**
- Automated registration
- Pre-recorded content
- Live Q&A at end
- Offer with limited availability

**When to Use:**
- Want consistency + live interaction
- Limited time for live events
- High-ticket benefit from live element
- Testing content before going live

**Best Practices:**
- Clear it's "mostly live" or hybrid
- Q&A is truly valuable
- Limited spots create urgency
- Follow up with no-shows

**Conversion Benchmark:** 12-25% attendees

---

## Event Funnels

### Purpose: Sell event registrations

#### 14. Live Event Funnel
**Structure:** Sales Page → Registration → Confirmation → Email Sequence → Event

**When to Use:**
- In-person events
- Virtual summits
- Limited capacity events
- High-value networking

**Best Practices:**
- Show social proof (past events)
- Clear what they'll learn
- Bonus for early registration
- Payment plan options
- Strong urgency (limited seats)

**Conversion Benchmark:** 2-8%

---

#### 15. Summit Funnel
**Structure:**
- Free Registration
- Confirmation + Upsell (VIP/All Access)
- Daily Sessions
- Offers during sessions

**When to Use:**
- Building audience quickly
- Multiple speakers/experts
- Content marketing at scale
- Backend offers for speakers

**Best Practices:**
- Strong speaker lineup
- Free with valuable recordings
- VIP upgrade with recordings + bonuses
- Each speaker has offer
- Automated follow-up

**Conversion Benchmark:** 30-50% to free, 5-15% to paid

---

## Advanced Funnels

### Purpose: Specific strategies for advanced marketers

#### 16. Invisible Funnel
**Structure:** Content with Embedded Offer → Inline Order Form → Thank You

**When to Use:**
- Warm audiences
- Content-first approach
- Avoiding "salesy" feel
- Native/social platform traffic

**Best Practices:**
- 80% value, 20% pitch
- Seamless transition to offer
- Context-relevant product
- Native payment options

**Conversion Benchmark:** Varies widely (0.5-5%)

---

#### 17. Hero Funnel
**Structure:** Brand/Bio Page → Lead Capture → Welcome Sequence → Core Offer

**When to Use:**
- Personal brand building
- Speakers/Authors/Influencers
- Long-term asset building
- Multiple funnels behind

**Best Practices:**
- Strong personal story
- Clear lead magnet
- Build relationship first
- Multiple backend funnels

**Conversion Benchmark:** 20-40%

---

#### 18. Storefront Funnel
**Structure:** Homepage → Product Categories → Product Pages → Cart → Checkout

**When to Use:**
- E-commerce stores
- Multiple products
- Repeat purchases
- Shopping-focused buyers

**Best Practices:**
- Easy navigation
- Clear product categories
- Reviews and social proof
- Abandoned cart sequence
- Upsells at checkout

**Conversion Benchmark:** 1-3%

---

#### 19. Cancellation Funnel
**Structure:** Cancel Request → Save Attempt → Thank You (or Cancellation)

**When to Use:**
- Subscription/membership programs
- Reducing churn
- Gathering feedback
- Last chance to save

**Best Practices:**
- One-click save option
- Discount offer for staying
- Feedback request
- Graceful exit if they cancel
- Win-back sequence

**Save Rate Benchmark:** 15-30%

---

#### 20. Affiliate Funnel
**Structure:** Application → Approval → Dashboard → Resources → Training

**When to Use:**
- Building affiliate army
- Scaling with partners
- Word-of-mouth amplification
- Performance-based marketing

**Best Practices:**
- Clear commission structure
- Marketing resources provided
- Training for success
- Tracking and reporting
- Contests and incentives

**Active Affiliate Benchmark:** 10-30% of applicants

## Funnel Selection Guide

### Quick Decision Tree

```
What's your primary goal?
├─ Generate Leads
│  ├─ Quick list building → Squeeze Page
│  ├─ Quality over quantity → Reverse Squeeze
│  ├─ Give value first → Lead Magnet
│  └─ Segment/customize → Survey/Bridge
│
├─ Sell Low-Mid Ticket (Under $500)
│  ├─ Story-driven → VSL Funnel
│  ├─ Text preference → Sales Letter
│  ├─ New customers → Tripwire Funnel
│  └─ Product launch → Launch Funnel
│
├─ Sell High-Ticket ($500+)
│  ├─ Educational sell → Webinar Funnel
│  ├─ One-on-one → Application Funnel
│  └─ Course launch → Product Launch
│
├─ Recurring Revenue
│  ├─ Membership site → Membership Funnel
│  ├─ SaaS product → Free Trial Funnel
│  └─ Subscription box → Physical Funnel
│
└─ Event/Experience
   ├─ Live event → Live Event Funnel
   ├─ Virtual summit → Summit Funnel
   └─ Workshop → Registration Funnel
```

## Funnel Stacking

### Advanced Strategy: Combine Multiple Funnels

**Example Full Stack:**
1. **Lead Funnel**: Squeeze page for lead magnet
2. **Nurture Sequence**: Email + retargeting
3. **Tripwire Funnel**: Low-ticket offer
4. **Webinar Funnel**: Core product presentation
5. **High-Ticket Funnel**: Application for coaching

**Key Principle**: Each funnel feeds the next, maximizing customer lifetime value.

## Optimization Priorities

### What to Optimize First

1. **Headline** (biggest impact)
2. **Offer** (value stack)
3. **Call to Action** (clarity and placement)
4. **Social Proof** (testimonials, results)
5. **Urgency/Scarcity** (real deadlines)
6. **Price/Payment Options**
7. **Guarantee**
8. **Design/Layout**

### Always Be Testing

- Split test one variable at a time
- Statistical significance (100+ conversions minimum)
- Document everything
- Scale winners, kill losers
