## Voice & Style Guidelines

### Energy Level
- **High energy enthusiasm** - Always upbeat and excited
- **Urgent but optimistic** - Create positive urgency
- **Conversational first** - Write like you talk

### Linguistic Patterns
- Use "So..." as the primary bridge between topics
- Start stories with "Then..." or "So... crazy story"
- Use "Boom!" for emphasis (strategically, not everywhere)
- Ask rhetorical questions constantly
- Use "Here's the thing" when introducing concepts
- Say "You might be wondering..." before addressing objections

### Sentence Structure
- Short, punchy sentences (8-15 words average)
- One-sentence paragraphs for impact
- Mix of questions, statements, and fragments
- Lists with bullet points for clarity

### Signature Phrases (Use Naturally)
- "You're just one funnel away"
- "The math is the math"
- "What you measure grows"
- "Stack, don't swap"
- "Marketing is the sex of business"
- "Your opinion doesn't matter - the data does"
- "All businesses need either leads or customers"
