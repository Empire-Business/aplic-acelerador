## Story Structures

### The Epiphany Bridge Story
```
[OPENING HOOK - relatable situation]

Hey, so I want to tell you a story about how I discovered [THE THING YOUR TEACHING].

[BACKSTORY - establish you were like them]
You know, back in [YEAR], I was [STRUGGLING WITH PAIN POINT]. I was trying to [THEIR CURRENT ATTEMPTS], but nothing seemed to work.

[EXTERNAL CONFLICT]
Then one day, [SPECIFIC EVENT] happened. It was painful because [EMOTIONAL IMPACT].

[INTERNAL STRUGGLE]
I felt [EMOTION]. I didn't know what to do. I tried [FAILED SOLUTIONS], but they didn't work.

[EPIPHANY MOMENT]
Then, almost by accident, I discovered [THE SOLUTION/NEW OPPORTUNITY].

[THE RESULT]
Suddenly, everything changed. [SPECIFIC RESULT]. And the craziest part? [UNEXPECTED BENEFIT].

[CALL TO ACTION]
And that's why I created [YOUR OFFER]. If you want to [BENEFIT], then [CTA].

Talk soon,
[Your Name]
P.S. [URGENCY/SCARCITY ELEMENT]
```

## Objection Handlers

### Price Objection
```
"I know what you might be thinking... 'This sounds great, but can I afford it?'

Here's the thing - when I was starting out, I was [MONEY STRUGGLE STORY]. But here's what I realized:

The cost of NOT solving this problem is [COST OF INACTION].

And actually, if you [DO THING], you'll [MAKE MONEY BACK].

Plus, with our guarantee, there's zero risk.

So the real question is: can you afford NOT to do this?"
```

### Time Objection
```
"You're busy - I get it. We're all busy.

But here's what I found: [TIME SAVING INSIGHT].

This isn't about adding MORE to your plate. It's about [EFFICIENCY BENEFIT].

And honestly, [TIMEFRAME] from now, you'll either have [RESULT] or you'll be in the same situation.

Which would you prefer?"
```

### Doubt Objection
```
"Look, I was skeptical too. When I first heard about [TOPIC], I thought [SKEPTICAL THOUGHT].

But then I tried it, and [RESULT].

Now, I'm not asking you to believe me. I'm asking you to try it for yourself.

And if you don't get [RESULT], I'll refund you. Fair?"
```

```

My name is [NAME], and over the next [TIME], I'm going to show you [BIG PROMISE].

But first, who is this for?
• Raise your hand if [QUALIFIER 1]
• How about if [QUALIFIER 2]
• And what about [QUALIFIER 3]

Perfect. If you raised your hand to any of those, you're in the right place.

By the end of this training, you're going to discover:
1. [LEARNING 1]
2. [LEARNING 2]
3. [LEARNING 3]

Sound good? Let's dive in!"
```

#### THE THREE SECRETS (30-40 minutes)

**Secret #1: The Vehicle**
```
"Okay, Secret #1: [SECRET NAME]

[TEACH THE SECRET - about 10 minutes]

Now, here's the thing - this is powerful, but it's not enough by itself..."

[STORY OF LEARNING THIS SECRET]

"Now, before I reveal Secret #2, I want to tell you a quick story..."

[TRANSITION STORY]
```

**Secret #2: Internal Beliefs**
```
"Secret #2: [SECRET NAME]

[TEACH THE SECRET - about 10 minutes]

Most people think the problem is [COMMON BELIEF]. But actually, it's [NEW BELIEF].

[PERSONAL STORY about this belief shift]

Let me show you exactly what I mean..."

[DEMONSTRATION]

"Now, when you combine Secret #1 and Secret #2, things start happening. But there's one more piece..."

[TRANSITION to Secret #3]
```

**Secret #3: The Stack/Method**
```
"Secret #3: [SECRET NAME]

This is where everything comes together.

[TEACH THE STEP-BY-STEP METHOD - about 15-20 minutes]

[DEMONSTRATE EACH STEP]

So when you put it all together:

• [Secret 2] gives you [BENEFIT 2]
• [Secret 3] gives you [BENEFIT 3]

And that's the complete system."
```

```
"Now, here's what usually happens next...

You learn something cool, you get excited, but then... life happens.

You get busy. You forget. You go back to your old patterns.

I don't want that for you.

I want you to actually get [RESULT].

That's why I created [OFFER NAME].

[PRESENT THE OFFER with STACK, not swap]

[INCLUDE BONUSES]

[ADD URGENCY/SCARCITY]

[INCLUDE GUARANTEE]

So here's what I want you to do right now...

Click the button below [DESCRIBE BUTTON].

Pick your [PACKAGE] option.

[BEGIN COUNTDOWN if applicable]
```

## Quick Copy Formulas

### AIDA (Classic)
1. **Attention** - Hook them immediately
2. **Interest** - Build curiosity
3. **Desire** - Show them what they want
4. **Action** - Tell them what to do

### PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solve)
1. **Problem** - Identify their pain
2. **Agitate** - Make the pain emotional
3. **Solve** - Present your solution

### FAB (Features-Advantages-Benefits)
1. **Feature** - What it is
2. **Advantage** - What it does
3. **Benefit** - What that means for them

### 4 P's (Picture-Promise-Prove-Push)
1. **Picture** - Paint the scenario
2. **Promise** - Show the transformation
3. **Prove** - Back it up with evidence
4. **Push** - Urgent call to action
